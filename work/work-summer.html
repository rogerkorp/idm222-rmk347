The Beginning of Summer

A rotoscope animation that experiments with color

This was a video I did as my final project for a design course. For this I filmed a basic recording of myself walking through a house, and rotoscoped the entire clip using Photoshop. I then finished the rest of the project in Premiere Pro, where I overlayed the animation on top of some other footage I filmed at around the same area.

The project was finished in June 2020
