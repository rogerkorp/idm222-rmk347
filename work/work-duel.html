Duel of the Dorks

A Star Wars Fan-film

This is a short lightsaber fight that I edited together using Premiere Pro and After Effects. The footage was shot over the course of a day during my 2020 Summer Break, but it was editied on-and-off over the course of several proceeding months. I used After Effects to manually rotoscope the lightsabers, and then I went into Premiere where I proceeded to edit in the Sound Effects and Soundtrack.

The short film was completed on September 27th, 2020.
