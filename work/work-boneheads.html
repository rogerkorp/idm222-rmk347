Attack of the Boneheads

Duel of the Dorks - Episode II

The sequel to "Duel of the Dorks". Attack of the Boneheads was edited together using Premiere Pro and After Effects. The footage was shot over the course of about two months during the Summer 2021. We used an iPhone X to record all of the footage, and it was all editied together using Premiere Pro and After Effects. SFX and greenscreen visuals were all sourced from free online resources. Title Sequences, lightsabers, and green screen effects were all created in After Effects

The short film was completed on September 26th, 2021.
