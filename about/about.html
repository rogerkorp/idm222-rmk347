About Me

My name is Roger Korpics

I’m a quirky UI, UX, and Graphic Designer, originally from the lovely coastal city of Newburyport, MA. I’m currently a full-time student at Drexel University in Philadelphia, PA, pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Interactive Digital Media.

In my spare time I enjoy expressing my ideas through designing, crafting, drawing, writing, and photography. For school assignments I develop user interfaces, prepare oral presentations, compose research reports, and develop my portfolio.

For my current work I rely on my MacBook Pro and the Adobe Creative Cloud. I primarily use Photoshop, Illustrator, and XD, in addition to Sketch, Flinto, and the Microsoft Office family of applications.
